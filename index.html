Our innovations

Human resource management

Our HRM solution streamlines post-recruitment process of an employee with features like self -boarding, GPS based attendance marking/Biometric Sensor based attendance marking, salary management, reports and document generation etc. so that employer can focus on his core business.

Agri Management

Our Agri Management Services includes all stages of crop plantation right from tillage to harvesting with application of Agricultural science & techniques to resolve various challenges faced by our modern farmers.

Agri IOT

Our IoT based smart farming solution automates your traditional farming and irrigation, all on your finger-tips. It helps in optimization of resources and improve crop yields. Our smart farming solution gathers farm data, analyses it and provide solution to enhance future productivity of farmlands.

Enterprise resource management

Our ERM solution releases the company management from the endless bunch of unorganised spreadsheets and organises them and stores the data on cloud, so that all users from CEO to Accountant to Godown managers can create and utilise the same data from anywhere in the world. Manage your inventory at various warehouses/ godowns, track inventory levels, accounting and much more with our ERM solution.

Supply Chain management

In today's sales & marketing world, a business adopts various channels like dealers, retailers, e-commerce, door-to-door selling, etc to sell. Our SCM application helps the sales & marketing managers manage multi-channel sales & distribution seamlessly. Sell through multiple channels, generate & scan bar-codes, manage sales and deliveries, etc. with SCM.
